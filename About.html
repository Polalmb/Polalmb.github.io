About Me

Born and raised on the south coast of NSW. I am of aboriginal and Irish descent, my mother’s family being from the Wiradjuri mob and my Father being of Irish heritage. I grew up in a town called batemans bay and I traveled around the state after highschool going from studying audio engineering in byron bay, living out the back of red rock mountain, to studying music in canberra. Although i'm not as adventurous as i used to be and spend a lot of time with family at home, I still always set aside plenty of time for one of my greatest passions. As silly as it may sound, I love to get a few close friends together, pack up the suby and head up into the mountains searching for gold.

My interest in IT

My interest in IT has always revolved around computers. When I was 7, we got our first computer at home which was amazing to me at the time as the school I attended had 50 students at the time and no computers besides what the teachers used. It was basically my first experience with the technology besides seeing computers in movies, I still remember not understanding how a mouse worked at the time.

It wasn't until I was 12 that we got internet for the first time ever. I remember being so amazed with google and what you could do by searching something that I decided I wanted to work with computers and work for google when I was older.

Since then I have always maintained a passion for computers and programming but never took it anywhere past being a hobby. I’ve worked in a few computer repair stores and did a bit of networking for my highschool but my interests slowly turned towards music for a long time until recently.

Why I chose RMIT

I found two Universities on OUA that interested me. RMIT and The University of South Australia as they both had IT courses that interested me. Ultimately it was my partner who helped me decide as she started university this year and told me she had sussed out RMIT and UOSA as a potential universities for herself and in her opinion RMIT was the best choice because in her opinion RMIT offered the most student support and the lecturers she had met during an open day were very professional but also very passionate about teaching

What I hope to learn with RMIT

During my study time with RMIT I hope to learn not only about IT and programming and everything else this course entails as it’s one of my passions, but I also hope to learn how to manage my time properly and Learn how to set a schedule and keep it because for the last year I have had more and more time on my hands my productivity and time management skills have deteriorated.
