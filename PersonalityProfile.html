Personality Profile

The Following Personality And Learning tests were taken for This assignment.

Myers-Briggs Test VARK Learning Style Test Apptitude Test

Myers-Briggs Test

The result from my Myers-Briggs test showed my personality type as; ISFP, Versatile Supporter

After reading through the information about ISFP personality types I feel as though it is a good representation of my personality. At my best I always try to keep a realistic mindset, Im dedicated and loyal to things I am passionate about and I hold strong to my ideals and values. At my worst I always feel as though there is too much going in my life to cope. I feel constrained for time and start to second guess myself.

I think my personality type will help me in team situations. Being able to take the reins when a team needs to work together and get everyone cooperating. These are important traits needed for team work, especially in an online university environment where students in a group will need to work hard to coordinate and get together as a team.

This can be taken into account when forming a team as I believe with my personality type and personal experience in previous university I have the skills and understanding needed to be able to lead a coordinated and function team that will work equally together.

Online Learning Test

The results for my online learning test showed that I am a very aural and hands on learner meaning I Learn better through listening to someone explain a concept or a task to me as well as learning through doing.

To me hands on learning is the best way I absorb information. Having worked with wood and metal for a lot of my life I have always learnt best by simply doing the task. In highschool I started making guitars. I still remember having no idea what I was doing nor did I understand the math involved with the task but with every guitar I made I learnt more and more about the precision involved with designing and making a body and neck mostly through trial and error. I remember reading about the math involved with placing a bridge and nut but it wasn't until I had put the math into practise on wood did I understand it.

When it comes to taking this learning style into account when working with a team I believe it would be more beneficial for me and my team for me to be someone who documents our interactions, discussions and brainstorms.

Mixed Aptitude test

The results for my mixed aptitude test showed that I was competent with numerical and word problems, inductive reasoning and abstract reasoning but incompetant when it comes to numerical logic and I can struggle to understand concepts. I expected that I wouldn't do very well when it came to numeracy as it is one area I have always struggled with throughout highschool was mathematics. I think these aspects may hinder me in a team situation as I can sometimes struggle to absorb information or to completely understand concepts and ideas in a group environment as I tend to absorb information slowly and may need to recount something several times to fully understand it.
